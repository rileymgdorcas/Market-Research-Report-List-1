<p><p>The Liquid Capacitive Inclinometer market is primarily divided into two types: Single Axis and Dual Axis Capacitive Inclinometers. Single Axis Capacitive Inclinometers measure tilt in one direction, making them ideal for applications requiring basic angle measurement. In contrast, Dual Axis Capacitive Inclinometers can measure tilt in two directions simultaneously, providing comprehensive data for complex applications. Both types leverage liquid capacitance technology to offer high precision and stability, making them essential in sectors such as construction, geotechnical monitoring, and civil engineering.</p></p>
