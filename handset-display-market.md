<p><p>The handset display market includes various technologies catering to diverse consumer needs. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) offers good color reproduction but limited viewing angles. LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays enhance brightness and efficiency, often used in backlighting. IPS (In-Plane Switching) provides superior color accuracy and wider viewing angles compared to standard LCD. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) delivers deeper blacks, vibrant colors, and flexibility for curved screens. Other types may include mini-LED or microLED, each with unique advantages for handheld devices.</p></p>
