<p><p>The electric fuel pumps market is typically categorized into two main types: internal electric fuel pumps and external electric fuel pumps. Internal electric fuel pumps are located inside the fuel tank, providing efficient fuel delivery while minimizing noise and heat exposure. In contrast, external electric fuel pumps are mounted outside the tank, offering easier access for maintenance and replacement. Both types play critical roles in modern automotive fuel systems, enhancing performance and reliability in fueling mechanisms across various vehicle models.</p></p>
