<p><p>The High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) geomembrane market comprises various types based on layering. Single-layer geomembranes are made of a single sheet, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness for applications like landfill liners. Double-layer geomembranes feature two sheets, enhancing durability and resistance to punctures, suitable for more demanding environments. Three-layer geomembranes incorporate an additional layer, often for improved chemical resistance and enhanced performance in specialized applications, such as mining and wastewater management, providing superior structural integrity and longevity.</p></p>
