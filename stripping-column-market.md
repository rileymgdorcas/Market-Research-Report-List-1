<p><ul><li>Packed Stripping Column</li><li>Tray Stripping Column</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stripping column markets include packed stripping columns and tray stripping columns, both of which are used in chemical processes to separate volatile components from liquid mixtures. Packed stripping columns utilize packing materials to enhance surface area for mass transfer, making them efficient for low-pressure operations. In contrast, tray stripping columns employ discrete trays to facilitate vapor-liquid contact, allowing for better control of the stripping process. Both types are essential in industries like petrochemicals and pharmaceuticals for optimizing separation efficiency.</p></p>
