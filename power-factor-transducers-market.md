<p><p>The Power Factor Transducers market is divided into two main types: AC Power Factor Transducers and DC Power Factor Transducers. AC Power Factor Transducers measure the phase difference between voltage and current in alternating current systems, helping optimize energy usage and improve efficiency. In contrast, DC Power Factor Transducers assess the efficiency of direct current systems, where power factor considerations are crucial for managing power quality. Both types play a vital role in enhancing electrical system performance across various applications.</p></p>
