<p><p>Titanium oxide target markets can be categorized into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets consist of flat, static surfaces used in processes like sputtering for thin film deposition, offering uniform material distribution. In contrast, rotating targets involve cylindrical designs that rotate during application, enhancing efficiency and material utilization by ensuring an even sputtering rate across the surface. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including electronics, optics, and solar energy, providing essential materials for advanced coatings and films.</p></p>
