<p><p>The Welding and Brazing Consumables market comprises two primary segments: welding consumables and brazing consumables. Welding consumables include electrodes, filler metals, and fluxes used in various welding processes such as arc, MIG, and TIG. They facilitate strong joints by melting and fusing materials together. Brazing consumables, on the other hand, involve filler metals that join materials without melting them, typically at lower temperatures. Both segments are essential in industries like construction, automotive, and manufacturing for ensuring robust and durable connections.</p></p>
