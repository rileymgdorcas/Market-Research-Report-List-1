<p><p>The adhesives handguns market consists of devices that apply adhesive materials, categorized primarily into hot melt and cold glue types. Hot melt handguns utilize thermoplastic adhesives, heating the material to a liquid state for application, providing rapid bonding and quick setting times. Cold glue handguns dispense water-based adhesives that remain in a liquid form until they evaporate or cure, ideal for applications requiring longer working times. Each type serves distinct markets, including packaging, woodworking, and crafts.</p></p>
