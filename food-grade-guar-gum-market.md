<p><p>The food-grade guar gum market is primarily divided into two types: powder and solution. Powdered guar gum is widely used as a thickening, stabilizing, and gelling agent in various food products, including sauces, dairy items, and gluten-free foods. Both forms derive from guar beans and are valued for their natural, plant-based properties in food formulations.</p></p>
