<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air-operated aluminum diaphragm pump market features two primary types: single-acting and double-acting pumps. Single-acting diaphragm pumps utilize air pressure to drive fluid through the discharge stroke, making them suitable for low-flow applications. In contrast, double-acting pumps operate in both directions, providing a more continuous flow by using air pressure for both the suction and discharge strokes, enhancing efficiency and capacity. Both types are widely used in industries such as chemicals, wastewater, and food processing for their reliability and versatility.</p></p>
