<p><p>The non-migratory plasticizer market is categorized into three main types: phthalates, trimellitates, and others. Phthalates are widely used for their effectiveness in enhancing flexibility and durability in various applications, especially in PVC products. Trimellitates, although less common, provide superior thermal stability and are ideal for high-performance applications. The "others" category includes alternative plasticizers like citrates and adipates, which are gaining popularity due to increasing health and environmental concerns associated with traditional plasticizers, driving innovation in sustainable materials.</p></p>
