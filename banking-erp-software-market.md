<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Banking ERP software is categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is hosted locally on a bank's servers, offering high levels of control and security but requiring substantial upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted remotely and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, lower initial costs, and easier updates. Banks choose between these types based on their operational needs, budget constraints, and desired flexibility in managing financial processes.</p></p>
