<p><p>The Car Night Vision System market comprises two primary types: Active Infrared Night Vision Systems and Passive Infrared Night Vision Systems. Active Infrared Night Vision Systems use an infrared light source to illuminate the environment, enhancing visibility during low-light conditions. In contrast, Passive Infrared Night Vision Systems detect and amplify existing ambient light, such as moonlight or starlight, to create enhanced images. Both systems aim to improve driver safety and awareness in nighttime or low-visibility driving conditions.</p></p>
