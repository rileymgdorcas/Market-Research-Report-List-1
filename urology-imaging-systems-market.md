<p><ul><li>Conventional Radiology Urology Imaging Systems</li><li>Ultrasound Urology Imaging Systems</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging</li><li>Computed Tomography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Urology Imaging Systems market comprises various types of imaging technologies used for diagnosing urinary tract conditions. Conventional Radiology Urology Imaging Systems rely on X-rays for initial assessments. Ultrasound Urology Imaging Systems utilize sound waves to create real-time images, offering a non-invasive option. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses powerful magnets and radio waves to produce detailed images, aiding in complex cases. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-ray images taken from different angles, providing 3D views for enhanced diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
