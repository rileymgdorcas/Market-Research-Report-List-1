<p><p>The tinted lenses market includes two primary types: fixed tint lenses and gradient tint lenses. Fixed tint lenses feature a uniform color throughout, providing consistent shading, which is ideal for activities requiring stable light reduction. Gradient tint lenses, on the other hand, have a color that fades from top to bottom, offering darker shading at the top and lighter at the bottom. This design allows for better light management while maintaining visibility, making them popular for fashion eyewear and driving glasses.</p></p>
