<p><p>Digital Twin technology encompasses various market types, including Parts Twin, Product Twin, Process Twin, and System Twin. Parts Twin refers to the digital representation of individual components, enabling precise monitoring and maintenance. Product Twin focuses on the entire product lifecycle, enhancing design and customer experience. Process Twin models operational processes for optimization and efficiency improvements. System Twin integrates various elements, assessing performance and interdependencies across larger systems, facilitating better decision-making and resource management in complex environments.</p></p>
