<p><p>The MEMS oscillator market is primarily divided into crystal oscillators and MEMS oscillators. Crystal oscillators utilize quartz crystals to maintain stable frequencies, offering high precision and reliability but are relatively bulky and fragile. In contrast, MEMS oscillators leverage microelectromechanical systems technology to produce compact, robust devices with low power consumption and greater resilience to environmental factors. MEMS oscillators are increasingly favored in portable electronics and telecommunications due to their small size and cost-effectiveness, driving growth in the market.</p></p>
