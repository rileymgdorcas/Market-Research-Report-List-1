<p><p>An Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer (OADM) is a device used in optical networks to add or drop specific wavelengths from a fiber optic line without disrupting other signals. The market for OADMs primarily includes two types: Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers, which have a predefined set of wavelengths for addition or removal, and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexers, which allow dynamic wavelength management to adapt to changing network demands. These technologies enhance network efficiency and flexibility in handling data traffic.</p></p>
