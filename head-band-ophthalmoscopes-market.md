<p><p>The headband ophthalmoscopes market includes two primary types of loupes: TTL (Through The Lens) loupes and flip-up loupes. TTL loupes are fixed and provide a streamlined design, offering a wide field of view with consistent optical alignment. They are ideal for users seeking precision and stability. Flip-up loupes, on the other hand, have adjustable lenses that can be flipped up when not in use, allowing for greater versatility and ease of switching between tasks. Both types enhance vision for ophthalmic examinations.</p></p>
