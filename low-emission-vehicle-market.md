<p><ul><li>Mild Hybrid Electric Vehicle</li><li>Hybrid Electric Vehicle</li><li>Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle</li><li>Pure Electric Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Emission Vehicle market includes several types: **Mild Hybrid Electric Vehicles (MHEVs)** use a small electric motor to assist the combustion engine, improving fuel efficiency. **Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs)** combine a combustion engine with an electric motor, relying on regenerative braking for energy. **Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs)** have larger batteries, allowing electric-only driving and recharging via external power. **Pure Electric Vehicles (EVs)** run solely on electricity, producing zero emissions, and rely entirely on charging infrastructure for power. Each type reduces emissions compared to traditional vehicles.</p></p>
