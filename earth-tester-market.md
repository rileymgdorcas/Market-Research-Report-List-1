<p><ul><li>Bench Top Earth Tester</li><li>Clamp on Earth Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Earth Tester market comprises primarily two types: Bench Top Earth Testers and Clamp-on Earth Testers. Bench Top Earth Testers are designed for stationary use, offering high precision for measuring soil resistivity and grounding systems, making them ideal for laboratory environments or field testing. In contrast, Clamp-on Earth Testers provide a portable, non-intrusive solution for measuring grounding resistance without disconnecting the system. These testers are favored for their ease of use and rapid measurements, catering to both professionals and industrial applications.</p></p>
