Water-borne paints use water as a primary solvent, making them more eco-friendly and popular due to lower VOC emissions. Other types may include specialty coatings and innovative formulations, catering to specific needs in the automotive sector. These variations enable customization based on performance, environmental impact, and application techniques.</p></p>
