<p><p>The servomotor gearboxes market consists of various types tailored for specific applications. Linear servo gearboxes convert rotational motion into linear movement, ideal for automation and robotics. They provide precise control and efficiency in applications requiring linear actuation. Right angle servo gearboxes, on the other hand, allow for the directional change of motion, enabling compact designs in systems where space is a constraint. Both types enhance performance and accuracy in various industrial and automation applications, catering to diverse engineering needs.</p></p>
