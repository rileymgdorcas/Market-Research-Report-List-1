<p><p>Pyro-Fuse market encompasses two primary types: blade fuses and glass tube fuses. Blade fuses are known for their compact design and are commonly used in automotive and electronic applications due to their reliability and ease of installation. In contrast, glass tube fuses offer a transparent casing, allowing for visual inspection of the fuse condition. They are widely used in household appliances and industrial equipment for their versatility and ability to handle various voltage ratings. Both types ensure effective circuit protection.</p></p>
