<p><p>The semiconductor cleaning device market consists primarily of two types: monolithic cleaning equipment and tank cleaning equipment. Monolithic cleaning equipment features a compact design that integrates various cleaning functions into a single unit, enhancing efficiency and space-saving. In contrast, tank cleaning equipment involves larger systems, using dedicated tanks to perform batch cleaning of semiconductor wafers or components, allowing for thorough cleaning of significant quantities simultaneously. Both types are essential for maintaining the cleanliness required in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
