UV absorbers absorb harmful ultraviolet light, and antiozonants shield polymers from ozone damage. Organosulfur compounds offer additional protection against environmental stressors. Together, these stabilizers play a crucial role in extending the lifespan and maintaining the quality of polymer products across various applications.</p></p>
