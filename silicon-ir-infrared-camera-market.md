<p><p>The Silicon IR Camera market is segmented based on wavelength ranges: </p><p>1. **Near Infrared (NIR)** cameras capture wavelengths from 0.75 to 1.4 micrometers, ideal for applications like nighttime surveillance and agricultural monitoring. </p><p>2. **Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)** operates between 1.4 to 3 micrometers, useful in industrial and scientific applications, including material analysis. </p><p>3. **Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)** spans 3 to 8 micrometers, widely used in military and thermal imaging. </p><p>4. **Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)** covers 8 to 14 micrometers, primarily for thermal imaging in building inspections and firefighting.</p></p>
