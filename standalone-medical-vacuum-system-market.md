<p><p>The standalone medical vacuum system market is segmented into three primary technologies: Dry Claw Pump Technology, which utilizes claw-shaped rotors to create a vacuum without lubrication; Dry Rotary Vane Technology, employing rotating vanes to achieve a vacuum in a contactless manner; and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology, which relies on oil for sealing and reducing wear, allowing for efficient operation. Each technology offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, maintenance, and application suitability in medical environments.</p></p>
