ISF type focuses on optimal properties for various applications, ensuring stability and performance. IHF type is tailored for industrial applications, providing enhanced viscosity and thermal stability suitable for machinery. ASF type is specialized for aerospace applications, offering superior heat resistance and lightweight characteristics, crucial for high-performance environments. Each type serves unique industrial needs.</p></p>
