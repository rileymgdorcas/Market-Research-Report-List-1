<p><ul><li>TTL IC (Transistor-transistor Logic)</li><li>CMOS Logic IC (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor)</li><li>BiCMOS Logic IC (Bipolar CMOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Standard Logic IC market comprises three main types: TTL, CMOS, and BiCMOS. TTL ICs, known for their speed and robustness, are widely used in digital circuits but consume more power. CMOS ICs are favored for their low power consumption and high density, making them ideal for battery-operated devices. BiCMOS combines the advantages of both technologies, offering high speed and low power, suitable for mixed-signal applications. Each type serves distinct roles in the electronics industry, reflecting varying performance and efficiency needs.</p></p>
