<p><ul><li>Single-screw Extruder</li><li>Twin-screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low pressure extruder market is primarily divided into two types: single-screw extruders and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders utilize a single helical screw within a barrel to process materials, offering simplicity and efficiency for various applications. In contrast, twin-screw extruders feature two intermeshing screws, enhancing mixing and conveying capabilities, making them ideal for complex formulations and polymer processing. Both types serve diverse industries, including food, plastics, and pharmaceuticals, catering to specific production requirements and material properties.</p></p>
