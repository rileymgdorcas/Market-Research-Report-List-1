<p><p>The Wi-Fi chipsets market is categorized into several types based on the IEEE standards they adhere to. 802.11n Wi-Fi chipsets offer improved speed and range through MIMO technology, catering to medium-speed applications. 802.11ac chipsets provide higher throughput with multi-user MIMO and beamforming, targeting high-definition streaming and gaming. 802.11ad chipsets utilize the 60 GHz band for ultra-fast data transfer over short distances, ideal for intensive tasks. The "Others" category includes emerging technologies and variations addressing specific needs in connectivity.</p></p>
