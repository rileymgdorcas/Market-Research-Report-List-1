<p><p>The analog oscilloscope market is primarily segmented into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel oscilloscopes capture one electrical signal, making them suitable for basic applications or troubleshooting simple circuits. In contrast, dual-channel oscilloscopes can simultaneously display two signals, allowing for comparative analysis and more complex measurements. This capability is essential for tasks such as timing analysis and signal interactions. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements and the complexity of the signals being analyzed.</p></p>
