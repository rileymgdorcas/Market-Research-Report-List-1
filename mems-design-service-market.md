<p><ul><li>Variable Capacitive Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS design service market encompasses various technologies, primarily including variable capacitive and piezoresistive types. Variable capacitive MEMS utilize changes in capacitance due to mechanical displacement, making them ideal for sensors and actuators in applications like pressure sensing and accelerometers. In contrast, piezoresistive MEMS rely on changes in electrical resistance when subjected to pressure or stress, commonly found in pressure sensors. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics, driving advancements in miniaturization and functionality.</p></p>
