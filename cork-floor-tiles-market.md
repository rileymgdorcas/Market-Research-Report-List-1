<p><strong>The Cork Floor Tiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Cork Tiles</li><li>Compressed Cork Tiles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cork floor tiles market is primarily divided into solid cork tiles and compressed cork tiles. Solid cork tiles are made from whole cork oak bark, offering natural insulation, cushioning, and durability, making them ideal for residential and commercial spaces. Compressed cork tiles, on the other hand, are produced by pressing cork granules together, creating a denser and more stable product. They are often used in high-traffic areas due to their enhanced durability while still providing the benefits of cork's natural properties.</p></p>
