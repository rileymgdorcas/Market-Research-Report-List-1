<p><p>Double coated urethane foam tapes come in three main types based on their adhesive formulations: acrylic-based, rubber-based, and silicone-based. Acrylic-based tapes offer excellent temperature resistance and UV stability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Rubber-based tapes provide strong adhesion and good conformability, ideal for general-purpose bonding. Silicone-based tapes are designed for high-temperature environments and are resistant to chemicals, making them suitable for specialized industrial applications. Each type caters to specific use cases, ensuring versatility and performance across various sectors.</p></p>
