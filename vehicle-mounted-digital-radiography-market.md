<p><strong>The Vehicle mounted Digital Radiography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle-mounted digital radiography market includes two primary types: CR (Computed Radiography) tech and DR (Digital Radiography) tech digital X-ray systems. CR tech uses photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, which are then scanned to produce digital radiographs, making it more portable and cost-effective. In contrast, DR tech employs flat-panel detectors for immediate image capture and processing, offering superior image quality and faster results, ideal for on-the-spot diagnostics in mobile healthcare settings like ambulances or field operations.</p></p>
