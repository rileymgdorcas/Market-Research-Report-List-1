<p><p>The ceiling fan market is primarily segmented into AC and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans are the traditional type, utilizing alternating current to operate, often offering a broader range of designs and lower upfront costs. In contrast, DC ceiling fans use direct current, providing higher energy efficiency and resulting in quieter operation. DC fans are generally lighter and can feature more advanced control options. As energy awareness grows, the market is increasingly leaning towards the adoption of DC ceiling fans.</p></p>
