<p><p>The laminated glass interlayer film market primarily consists of two key types: EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) and PVB (polyvinyl butyral) interlayer films. EVA films are known for their flexibility and UV resistance, making them ideal for architectural and automotive applications. PVB films, on the other hand, provide excellent adhesion and impact resistance, commonly used in safety glass for buildings and vehicles. Both types enhance the strength and durability of laminated glass, catering to specific needs in construction and automotive sectors.</p></p>
