<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft market features three primary types: Tilt-rotors, Lift+Cruise, and Multi-rotors. Tilt-rotor designs utilize rotating blades for vertical lift, transitioning to horizontal flight with wing-assisted lift. Lift+Cruise aircraft separate lift and cruise functionalities, typically using fixed wings for efficient travel. Multi-rotors feature multiple rotors for vertical takeoff and landing, ideal for short-range urban mobility. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to diverse applications in urban air mobility, logistics, and personal transport.</p></p>
