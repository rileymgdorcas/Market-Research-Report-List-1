<p><strong>The Peristaltic Pumps for Municipal Water and Waste Water Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps are essential in municipal water and wastewater applications, employing a mechanism that compresses and relaxes a flexible tube to move fluids. Roller peristaltic pumps use rollers to create pressure on the tube, providing high flow rates and precise control, ideal for transporting viscous fluids. In contrast, shoe-type peristaltic pumps utilize a shoe mechanism for gentle handling, making them suitable for slurries and shear-sensitive materials. Both types ensure minimal contamination and easy maintenance in demanding environments.</p></p>
