<p><strong>The Flash-Based Array Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>All-flash Array</li><li>Hybrid Flash Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flash-based array market is primarily divided into two types: All-Flash Arrays (AFAs) and Hybrid Flash Arrays (HFAs). All-Flash Arrays consist entirely of flash memory, offering high performance, low latency, and energy efficiency, making them ideal for data-intensive applications. In contrast, Hybrid Flash Arrays combine flash storage with traditional hard disk drives (HDDs), balancing performance and cost-effectiveness. HFAs provide greater capacity at a lower price point, making them suitable for businesses with varying storage needs and budgets.</p></p>
