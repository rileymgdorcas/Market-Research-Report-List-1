<p><p>The control valve mechanism market comprises three main types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic control valves. Pneumatic control valves utilize compressed air for operation, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Electric control valves rely on electric actuators for precise control, offering versatility and integration with automation systems. Hydraulic control valves operate using pressurized fluids, ideal for high-pressure environments where robust force is required. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, contributing to efficient process control across various sectors.</p></p>
