<p><p>The marine steering gear system market includes three primary types: manual steering, hydraulic steering, and electric power steering. Manual steering relies on human effort for helm control, offering simplicity and reliability. Hydraulic steering utilizes fluid pressure to enhance steering precision and reduce effort, making it suitable for larger vessels. Electric power steering employs electric motors for seamless steering control, optimizing energy efficiency and offering advanced features like automated assistance. Each type caters to different vessel sizes and operational requirements, enhancing navigational performance.</p></p>
