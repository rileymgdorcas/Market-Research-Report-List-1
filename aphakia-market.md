<p><p>The aphakia market is categorized into three main types: congenital aphakia, surgical aphakia, and acquired aphakia. Congenital aphakia refers to the absence of the lens present at birth, often associated with genetic conditions. Surgical aphakia arises from lens removal due to cataract surgery or trauma. Acquired aphakia develops later in life, typically resulting from trauma or disease affecting the eye. Each type presents unique causes and treatment approaches, influencing market dynamics for corrective solutions and innovations.</p></p>
