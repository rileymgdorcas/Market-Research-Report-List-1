<p><p>The 3D printing devices market is divided into two main types: metal printing and non-metal printing. Metal printing utilizes processes like selective laser melting and electron beam melting to create high-strength components for industries such as aerospace and automotive. Non-metal printing encompasses various materials, including plastics, ceramics, and composites, often used in prototyping and consumer products. Both segments cater to diverse applications, with metal printing focusing on durability and performance, while non-metal printing emphasizes versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
