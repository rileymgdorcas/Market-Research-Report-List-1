<p><p>The consumer MEMS microphones market is divided into two main types: analog and digital microphones. Analog MEMS microphones output a continuous electrical signal proportional to sound waves, commonly used in basic audio applications. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones process sound into a digital signal, offering improved noise resistance and higher fidelity, making them ideal for smartphones, tablets, and voice-activated devices. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing performance, cost, and application versatility.</p></p>
