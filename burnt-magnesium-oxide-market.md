<p><ul><li>Lightly Burnt Powder</li><li>Reburned Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The burnt magnesium oxide market consists of two main types: lightly burnt powder and reburned powder. Lightly burnt powder is produced through a controlled calcination process, resulting in a product with lower reactivity, making it suitable for applications like ceramics and refractories. In contrast, reburned powder undergoes a secondary calcination, enhancing its purity and reactivity, which is ideal for high-performance applications such as in pharmaceuticals and advanced materials. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on reactivity and application requirements.</p></p>
