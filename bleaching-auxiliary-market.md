<p><p>Bleaching auxiliaries are crucial in the textile industry for enhancing the whitening process. The market can be segmented into three types: anionic, cationic, and nonionic. Anionic auxiliaries, which carry a negative charge, are effective in alkaline conditions and help in removing stains. Cationic auxiliaries, with a positive charge, are used to impart softness and improve dye uptake, especially in acidic environments. Nonionic auxiliaries, having no charge, are versatile and suitable for various applications, offering stability across different pH levels.</p></p>
