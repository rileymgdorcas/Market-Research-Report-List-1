<p><p>The **Commercial Vehicle Bearings Market** includes **Ball Bearings** and **Roller Bearings**, essential for smooth vehicle operation. **Ball Bearings** use spherical balls to reduce friction, ideal for lighter loads and high-speed applications like wheels and transmissions. **Roller Bearings** employ cylindrical or tapered rollers, handling heavier loads and stress, commonly used in axles and gearboxes. Both types enhance durability, efficiency, and performance in commercial vehicles, catering to diverse load and speed requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
