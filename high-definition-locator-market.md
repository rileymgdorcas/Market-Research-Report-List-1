<p><p>The High-definition Locator market is segmented into two main types: GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) and INS (Inertial Navigation System). GNSS involves satellite-based positioning, offering high accuracy for various applications like navigation and mapping. In contrast, INS utilizes internal sensors to track position, velocity, and orientation, making it suitable for environments where satellite signals may be weak or unavailable. Both systems are essential for precision in fields such as automotive, aerospace, and augmented reality, enhancing overall navigation capabilities.</p></p>
