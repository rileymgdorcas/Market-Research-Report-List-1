<p><p>The industrial clutches and brakes market encompasses various types that cater to specific operational needs. Electric clutches and brakes utilize electrical energy for actuation, offering precise control and response. Mechanical options rely on physical connections, providing robustness for heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic systems use compressed air for actuation, suitable for environments requiring rapid response.
