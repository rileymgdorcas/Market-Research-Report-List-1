<p><strong>The Power Tool Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Motor</li><li>AC Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power tool motor market includes two primary types: DC motors and AC motors. DC motors are favored for their precise speed control and torque delivery, making them ideal for battery-operated tools. They offer high efficiency and compact designs, suitable for handheld applications. In contrast, AC motors are commonly used in fixed power tools due to their durability and ability to handle higher power loads. They perform efficiently in continuous operation, making them suitable for industrial applications.</p></p>
