<p><ul><li>Phase Burner</li><li>Self-recirculating Burner</li><li>Rich-lean Burner</li><li>Split Flame Type Burner</li><li>Hybrid Accelerated Burner</li><li>Low NOx Preburner Chamber Burner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Low Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) Burner market includes various types designed for reduced emissions and enhanced efficiency. Phase burners operate by adjusting fuel and air ratios to control combustion. Self-recirculating burners recycle flue gas to lower temperatures, minimizing NOx formation. Rich-lean burners use fuel-rich and fuel-lean stages to optimize combustion. Split flame type burners create two distinct flames, reducing peak temperatures. Hybrid accelerated burners combine multiple techniques for efficient combustion, while low NOx preburner chamber burners reduce emissions before main combustion.</p></p>
