Push-pull cables are designed to transmit movement in both directions, allowing for precise control of aircraft or machinery components. In contrast, pull-pull cables operate by pulling in one direction, often used for actuating systems where space is limited. Both types are crucial for ensuring reliable and efficient operation of military and aviation equipment, enhancing their performance and safety.</p></p>
