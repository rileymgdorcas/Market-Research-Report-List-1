<p><p>The reclaimed rubber market encompasses several key types, including Waste Tire Rubber (WTR), Butyl Reclaim, and EPDM reclaim. WTR, derived from recycled tires, is widely used in manufacturing new rubber products. Butyl Reclaim, sourced from the recycling of butyl rubber, is valued for its impermeability and elasticity, making it ideal for applications like inner tubes. EPDM reclaim, obtained from recycled ethylene propylene diene monomer, is known for its excellent weather resistance, finding uses in roofing and automotive sectors.</p></p>
