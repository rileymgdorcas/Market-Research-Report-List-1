<p><ul><li>Single-Phase Motor Protection Relays</li><li>Three-Phase Motor Protection Relays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor protection relays market is categorized into two primary types: single-phase and three-phase motor protection relays. Single-phase motor protection relays are designed for smaller, less complex systems, primarily protecting single-phase motors from overloads and faults. In contrast, three-phase motor protection relays cater to larger, industrial applications, safeguarding three-phase motors from similar issues while providing advanced features like phase imbalance detection and overcurrent protection. Both types are crucial for ensuring efficient motor operation and preventing potential damage.</p></p>
