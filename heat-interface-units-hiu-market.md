The market is divided into two main types: indirect HIUs, which utilize a heat exchanger to separate the heating fluid from potable water, ensuring safe consumption, and direct HIUs, which circulate the heating fluid directly for heating water and spaces. Both types optimize energy efficiency and provide tailored heating solutions for different applications and requirements.</p></p>
