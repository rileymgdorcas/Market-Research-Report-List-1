Straight Seam Welded Pipe is produced by welding two metal edges together along a straight line, ensuring structural integrity for high-pressure applications. In contrast, Helical Welded Pipe features a spiral seam, allowing for enhanced flexibility and strength, often used in pipelines and transportation structures. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
