Electromagnetic door locks utilize magnetic fields to secure the door, providing reliable locking mechanisms favored for their robustness. Electric motor door locks, on the other hand, use a small motor to engage or disengage the locking mechanism, allowing for smoother operation and integration with central locking systems.
