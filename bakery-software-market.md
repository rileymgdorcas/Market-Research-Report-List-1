<p><p>The bakery software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software allows bakeries to access their systems online, offering flexibility, remote access, and automatic updates. This model typically involves a subscription fee and minimizes the need for extensive IT infrastructure. In contrast, on-premises software is installed directly on local systems, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and preferences within the bakery industry.</p></p>
