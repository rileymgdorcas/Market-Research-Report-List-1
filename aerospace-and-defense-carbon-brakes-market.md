LPI, on the other hand, uses liquid precursors to infiltrate carbon fibers, offering cost-effective production and enhanced mechanical properties. Both methods cater to the demanding requirements of aerospace and defense applications, ensuring reliability and efficiency under extreme conditions.</p></p>
