<p><ul><li>Self-erecting Tower Cranes</li><li>Flat Top Tower Cranes</li><li>Hammerhead Tower Cranes</li><li>Luffing Jib Tower Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium tower crane market includes several types, each designed for specific construction needs. Self-erecting tower cranes are compact and ideal for smaller projects due to their ease of setup and mobility. Flat top tower cranes offer a versatile design, minimizing height restrictions and improving visibility. Hammerhead tower cranes are suited for larger projects, providing high lifting capabilities with a robust structure. Luffing jib tower cranes excel in tight spaces, allowing for greater maneuverability and reduced risk of obstruction during operation.</p></p>
