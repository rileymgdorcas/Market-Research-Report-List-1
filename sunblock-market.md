<p><ul><li>Physical Sunblock</li><li>Chemical Sunblock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sunblock market is generally divided into two types: physical and chemical sunblocks. Physical sunblocks, also known as mineral sunblocks, contain active mineral ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide, which sit on the skin's surface and reflect UV rays. They are often preferred for sensitive skin. Chemical sunblocks, on the other hand, contain organic compounds that absorb UV radiation and convert it into heat, which is then released from the skin. Each type caters to different skin types and preferences.</p></p>
