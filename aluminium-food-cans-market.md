<p><ul><li>Three-Piece Cans</li><li>Two-piece Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum food cans market comprises primarily two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans consist of a body made from a cylindrical sheet, with a top and bottom welded or seamed to the sides. This design is often used for foods needing higher durability. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are crafted from a single sheet of aluminum that’s formed into a can body with a separate lid. These cans are generally lighter, cost-effective, and preferred for beverages and lightweight food products.</p></p>
