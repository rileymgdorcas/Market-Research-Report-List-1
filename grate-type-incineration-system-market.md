<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Grate Stoker</li><li>Step Grate Stoker</li><li>Roller Grate Stoker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grate-type incineration system market includes three main types: reciprocating grate stoker, step grate stoker, and roller grate stoker. </p><p>Reciprocating grate stokers feature a moving bed that alternates direction, promoting thorough waste combustion. Step grate stokers use a series of steps to transport waste while allowing efficient air circulation. Roller grate stokers utilize rotating rollers to facilitate continuous waste feeding and combustion. Each type offers distinct advantages in efficiency, waste handling, and emissions control, catering to varied industrial and municipal waste management needs.</p></p>
