<p><p>Medical device validation and verification encompass various market types essential for ensuring device safety and efficacy. Mechanical testing evaluates durability and performance under mechanical stress. Biological testing assesses compatibility and safety against biological systems. EMC (Electromagnetic Compatibility) and ECI (Electromagnetic Interference) testing ensure devices operate reliably in their electromagnetic environment. Electrical safety testing confirms that devices do not pose electrical hazards. Together, these testing types ensure that medical devices meet stringent regulatory requirements and protect patient health.</p></p>
