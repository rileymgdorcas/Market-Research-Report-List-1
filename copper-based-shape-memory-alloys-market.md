<p><p>The Copper Based Shape Memory Alloys (Cu-SMAs) market is segmented into high-temperature austenite phase and low-temperature martensite phase. The high-temperature austenite phase is characterized by its ability to retain shape memory and exhibit mechanical stability at elevated temperatures, making it suitable for automotive and aerospace applications. In contrast, the low-temperature martensite phase is highly flexible and can be activated at lower temperatures, often used in biomedical implants and consumer products. Each phase serves distinct industrial needs, driving market growth.</p></p>
