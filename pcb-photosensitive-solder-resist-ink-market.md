<p><strong>The PCB Photosensitive Solder Resist Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Photosensitive</li><li>Uv Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB photosensitive solder resist ink market primarily comprises two types: liquid photosensitive and UV cured inks. Liquid photosensitive inks are viscous materials applied to PCBs, requiring a drying process to harden after exposure to light. In contrast, UV cured inks solidify instantly when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering rapid processing and enhanced durability. Both types serve to protect circuit boards during manufacturing, ensuring precision in soldering and improving the overall reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
