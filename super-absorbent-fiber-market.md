<p><p>The Super Absorbent Fiber (SAF) market is categorized into two primary types: short staple fiber grade and long staple fiber grade. Short staple fiber grade is characterized by shorter lengths, making it suitable for products requiring quick absorption and bulkiness, such as diapers and feminine hygiene products. In contrast, long staple fiber grade offers enhanced strength and durability, often used in applications requiring higher performance, such as medical supplies and industrial absorbents. Both grades cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
