<p><p>The laparoscopic clips market consists of two primary types: polymeric absorbable clips and metallic clips. Polymeric absorbable clips are designed to dissolve over time, reducing long-term complications and providing a temporary fixation solution in surgical procedures. In contrast, metallic clips remain permanently in the body, offering durable fixation for tissues and vessels during laparoscopic surgeries. Both types are essential in minimally invasive procedures, enhancing surgical efficiency and patient recovery while addressing different clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
