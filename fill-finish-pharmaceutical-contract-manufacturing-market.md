Cooperative Manufacturing involves collaboration between pharmaceutical companies and contract manufacturers, sharing resources and expertise for production efficiency. Outsource All refers to fully delegating the filling and finishing processes to contract manufacturers, allowing companies to focus on core activities. Hybrid models combine both strategies, utilizing contract manufacturing for certain aspects while retaining control over critical processes, promoting flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
