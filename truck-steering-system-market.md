<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electric Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck steering system market includes three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electric Power Steering (EPS), and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). HPS uses hydraulic pressure to assist steering, offering reliability but lower fuel efficiency. EPS relies on electric motors for assistance, providing better fuel economy and precision. EHPS combines hydraulic and electric systems, balancing efficiency and performance. Each system caters to different trucking needs, with HPS being robust, EPS more modern and efficient, and EHPS offering a hybrid solution for enhanced control and energy savings.</p></p>
