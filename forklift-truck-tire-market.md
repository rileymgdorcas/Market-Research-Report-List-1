<p><p>The forklift truck tire market is segmented into three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic forklift tires feature air-filled designs that provide cushioning and traction, suitable for rough terrains. Solid forklift tires are made from durable rubber, offering puncture resistance and maintenance-free operation, making them ideal for indoor and smooth surfaces. Polyurethane forklift tires are lightweight and designed for high-performance applications, offering excellent wear resistance and a lower rolling resistance, enhancing energy efficiency in industrial settings.</p></p>
