<p><p>The LTE IoT market primarily comprises two technologies: LTE-M and NB-IoT. LTE-M (Long Term Evolution for Machines) offers higher data rates and supports mobility, making it suitable for applications like asset tracking and connected vehicles. Conversely, NB-IoT (Narrowband IoT) focuses on providing extensive coverage with lower power consumption, ideal for stationary applications such as smart meters and environmental monitoring. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for scalable and efficient IoT connectivity across various industries.</p></p>
