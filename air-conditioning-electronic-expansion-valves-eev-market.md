<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are crucial components in air conditioning systems, regulating refrigerant flow to optimize performance and enhance energy efficiency. The market for EEVs is primarily segmented into two types: electromagnetic EEVs and electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs utilize electromagnetic coils to control the valve opening, offering precise flow control and rapid response. In contrast, electric EEVs operate using electric motors, enabling advanced integration with modern control systems, which allows for more accurate modulation and improved system efficiency.</p></p>
