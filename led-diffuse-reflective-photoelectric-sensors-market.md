<p><p>LED diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors are utilized for detecting objects by emitting light and measuring the reflection. In the market, these sensors are categorized into two main types based on their output signals: digital and analog. Digital signal sensors produce a binary output, indicating presence or absence of an object, ideal for discrete applications. In contrast, analog signal sensors offer a continuous output proportional to the intensity of the reflected light, providing more detailed information for varied industrial applications.</p></p>
