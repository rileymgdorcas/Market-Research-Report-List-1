<p><ul><li>Anode Binder</li><li>Cathode Binder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium binders market comprises two main types: anode binders and cathode binders. Anode binders enhance the structural integrity and conductivity of anodes in lithium-ion batteries, typically supporting materials like graphite. Conversely, cathode binders are used to stabilize and connect active materials in cathodes, aiding in energy retention and overall battery performance. Both types play crucial roles in improving battery efficiency, cycle life, and safety, driving advancements in electric vehicles and portable electronic devices.</p></p>
