<p><p>Solution Polymerized Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (S-SBR) is produced through two main processes: batch and continuous. The batch S-SBR process involves polymerizing the monomers in a series of sequential steps, allowing control over the molecular weight and composition but often requiring more time and energy. In contrast, the continuous S-SBR process operates in a single, uninterrupted flow, enhancing efficiency and reducing production costs. Both methods cater to demand in tire manufacturing and other applications requiring high-performance rubber.</p></p>
