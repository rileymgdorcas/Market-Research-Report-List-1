<p><strong>The Low Light Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active IR Focus</li><li>CCD AF</li><li>Phase Difference Measurement Focus</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low light cameras market features three primary focus technologies. Active IR focus utilizes infrared light to enhance focusing in dim conditions, ideal for night vision applications. CCD AF (Charge-Coupled Device Autofocus) employs a sensor-based approach that captures detailed low-light images but can struggle with speed. Phase difference measurement focus uses advanced algorithms to quickly detect focus by comparing phase differences in light, ensuring faster and more accurate autofocus performance in low-light environments. Each type serves distinct needs in low-light photography.</p></p>
