<p><p>The transportable ventilators market is categorized into invasive and non-invasive ventilation types. Invasive ventilation involves the use of tubes or artificial airways that directly connect to the patient's airway, providing precise respiratory support, often used in critical care settings. Non-invasive ventilation, on the other hand, offers respiratory assistance without invasive procedures, employing masks or nasal interfaces to deliver air. Both types are crucial in emergency transport, enabling effective patient management in various healthcare scenarios.</p></p>
