<p><strong>The Tri-band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tri-band wireless router market encompasses various Wi-Fi standards, each catering to different needs. The 802.11a offers high speeds over the 5 GHz band, while 802.11b operates on the 2.4 GHz band, providing greater range but slower speeds. 802.11g combines both, enhancing speed with 2.4 GHz compatibility. The 802.11n improves performance with multiple antennas and supports both frequencies. Lastly, 802.11ac significantly boosts speed and capacity using wider channels and advanced technology, ideal for high-demand environments.</p></p>
