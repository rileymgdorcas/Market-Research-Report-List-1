<p><p>The radiator hose market is primarily divided into two types: molded and flexible. Molded hoses are custom-shaped to fit specific engine designs, ensuring leak-free connections and optimal coolant flow. They are often made from durable materials to withstand high temperatures and pressures. In contrast, flexible hoses offer versatility, allowing for easier installation and adaptability to various engine layouts. These hoses are typically made of rubber or silicone, providing excellent resistance to heat and chemicals while accommodating movement and vibrations.</p></p>
