<p><ul><li>Exterior Mirrors</li><li>Interior Mirrors</li><li>Under Rearview Mirrors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive rear-view camera market can be categorized into three primary types: exterior mirrors, interior mirrors, and under rearview mirrors. Exterior mirrors are mounted on the vehicle's sides and provide a wide-angle view of the surroundings, enhancing safety during maneuvers. Interior mirrors, integrated within the cabin, offer a rearview display to assist drivers in monitoring rear traffic. Under rearview mirrors are discreetly positioned for optimal visibility and can include additional features like sensors, improving overall driver awareness and safety.</p></p>
