<p><p>The Single Mode Broadband Fiber Coupler market consists of various types based on the number of outputs, including 1x2, 1x4, 1x8, 1x16, and 1x32 configurations. A 1x2 coupler splits a single input fiber into two outputs, while a 1x4 divides it into four. Similarly, 1x8, 1x16, and 1x32 models continue this trend, allowing for increased connectivity options in optical networks. Each type serves different applications, catering to specific network demands and scalability requirements.</p></p>
