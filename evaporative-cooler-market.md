<p><p>An evaporative cooler, also known as a swamp cooler, is a device that cools air through the evaporation of water. Utilizing a simple yet effective mechanism, these coolers draw in warm air, pass it through wet pads, and release cooler, more humidified air into the environment. They are energy-efficient and environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional air conditioning systems, especially in dry climates.</p><p>The evaporative cooler market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient cooling solutions and rising awareness of environmental sustainability. As global temperatures rise, consumers are actively seeking cost-effective cooling options, fueling the adoption of evaporative coolers in residential and commercial settings.
