<p><p>The Ethylene Propylene Diene Terpolymer (EPDM) market is segmented based on polymerization methods: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves dissolving monomers in a solvent, providing better control over molecular weight and polymer properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Suspension polymerization, on the other hand, disperses monomers in a continuous phase, leading to larger particle sizes and a more straightforward separation process. Both methods cater to diverse industries like automotive, construction, and consumer goods, influencing product characteristics and market dynamics.</p></p>
