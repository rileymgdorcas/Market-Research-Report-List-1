<p><p>The laboratory pH sensor market primarily consists of three types: glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors are traditional, utilizing a glass membrane to measure pH levels through the potential difference in hydrogen ion concentration. ISFET sensors, based on ion-sensitive field effect transistor technology, provide faster response times and are more durable. Other types may include solid-state sensors and optical sensors, each offering unique advantages and applications in various laboratory settings, enhancing pH measurement versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
