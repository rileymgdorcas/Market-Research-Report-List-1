<p><p>The 3D printing of metals market comprises various technologies, including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a laser to selectively fuse metal powder, allowing for high-resolution and complex geometries. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, making it ideal for thick components and high melting point metals. Other methods include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, which offer alternative approaches to metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
